How do I get an offer listed on OSMC Market?
Create a Chest Shop in the Mall or Market. The trade details will be detected automatically and added to the page.
My offer isn’t showing up or is showing 0 stock.
Check the ‘Last Updated’ time in the header. If you’re sure your trade isn’t visible or is incorrect, please email the details to wavpainter@gmail.com.
Why are Buy and Sell swapped compared to in-game?
The trades are presented here as offers, so it’s more intuitive to list the trades from the shop’s point-of-view. In-game, the offers are presented from the player/customer point-of-view.
Sell means “the shop is selling the item”.
Buy means “the shop is buying the item”.
